SOFT PNEUMATIC VALVE

Pneumatic Valve, Thermoplastic sheet

Date

1 March. 2018

Category

Interaction Design

Contributers

Purnendu, Kristian Gohlke,
Prof. Eva Hornecker

The soft pneumatic valve is a geometrical construction fabricated by heat sealing layers of soft thermoplastic sheets. Without involving any hard plastic or metallic part, a valve or 'pneumatic diode' has been contructed out of soft thermoplastic sheets. It builds upon the powerful idea that information is physical in nature. So carefully designed geometries can represent basic computational entities like a diode or switch going beyond conventional electronics. [More details restricted]

The major part of this work was done at the Bauhaus University Weimer working under supervision of Prof. Eva Hornecker while some minor work was done at IIT Roorkee.
